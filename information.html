WHAT DOES MUSIC TRULLY MEANS?


Vocal or instrumental sounds (or both) combined in such a way as to produce beauty of form, harmony, and expression of emotion.

INSTRUMENTS

A musical instrument is a device or object used to create or produce sounds and is played for musical purposes.

MELODY AND HARMONY

Melody is a sequence of musical notes. While harmony refers to the combination of multiple notes to create a pleasing sound.

DYNAMICS

Dynamics in music refers to the variations in volume, intensity, and expression, adding depth and emotion to the performance.

RHYTHM AND TEMPO

Rhythm is the pattern of beats and accents in music, while tempo refers to the speed or pace at which the music is played.

TYPES OF INSTRUMENTS


There are five types of musical instruments: percussion, string, brass, woodwind, and keyboard. The categories are based on how the instruments produce their respective sounds.

PERCUSSION

Drums, cymbals, tambourines, maracas, bongos, congas, xylophones, cowbells, triangles, timpani, gongs, snare, and various other instruments create percussion.

STRING

A symphony of melodies, weaving notes with rhythm, evoking emotions and stories, creating a harmonious tapestry that resonates deep within the soul.

BRASS

Brass music refers to a genre of music that features instruments made of brass, such as trumpets and trombones.

WOODWIND

Woodwind instruments are a family of musical instruments that produce sound by blowing air through a mouthpiece or reed.

KEYBOARD

The keyboard is a versatile musical instrument with a set of keys that produce sound when pressed, often used in various genres of music.
